Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

TikTokGrab collects the following information:

  • Video metadata for download functionality
  • User preferences for download settings
  • Storage organization preferences

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used exclusively for:

  • Processing video downloads
  • Implementing user preferences
  • Organizing downloaded content

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No personal information is collected or stored
  • Data can be cleared at any time through extension settings

4. Chrome Permissions

TikTokGrab requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with TikTok video pages
  • downloads: To save videos to your device
  • storage: To save your preferences
  • Host permissions: Limited to TikTok.com domains
